## Content Strategy

### Pillar Topics (4 pillars, ~10 posts each)

| Pillar | Theme | Internal Link Target |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| **Pillar 1** — "Best POS for [X]" | Commercial intent — buyers comparing options | `/pricing`, `/download` |
| **Pillar 2** — "How to / Guide" | Informational intent — building topical authority | `/features`, `/support` |
| **Pillar 3** — "POS for [Industry]" | Industry-specific — narrow, high-conversion | `/industries`, `/download` |
| **Pillar 4** — "ZyntaPOS How-to" | Branded intent — retention + new user onboarding | `/support`, `/download` |

### On-Page SEO Rules (apply to every post)

- **Title tag:** 50–60 chars, primary keyword near front
- **Meta description:** 145–160 chars, include keyword + CTA phrase
- **H1:** Matches or closely mirrors title tag keyword
- **First 100 words:** Primary keyword appears naturally
- **H2 subheadings:** Secondary and LSI keywords
- **Internal links:** Min 2 per post → `/download`, `/pricing`, `/features`, or another blog post
- **CTA at end:** Always link to `/download`
- **Word count:** 800–1,500 words (4,000+ characters)
- **Schema:** Article JSON-LD via BlogPost layout (already implemented)
