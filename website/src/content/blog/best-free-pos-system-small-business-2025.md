## What Makes a POS System "Good" for Small Business?

Not all POS software is built the same. For a small retailer, restaurant, or service business, the right system must hit these marks:

- **Works offline** — internet outages shouldn't stop sales
- **Runs on affordable hardware** — Android tablets beat expensive proprietary terminals
- **Includes real inventory management** — not just a checkout button
- **Transparent pricing** — no hidden fees or surprise charges
- **Easy to set up** — no IT department required

## The Problem with Most "Free" POS Systems

Most systems advertised as free are free only in name. The hidden costs appear as:

- **Per-transaction fees** (0.5%–2.6% per sale adds up fast)
- **Paid add-ons for basic features** like reports or multi-user access
- **Hardware lock-in** requiring proprietary card readers or tablets
- **Cloud-only operation** — if your internet drops, so does your POS

A genuinely free POS system gives you the core features without forcing upgrades or charging transaction fees.
